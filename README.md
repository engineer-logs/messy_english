# 📘 The 180-Day English Mastery System
**Convert messy notes into polished learning materials using AI.**

## 🎯 System Highlights
* ✅ **Note Transformation**: Converts raw subtitle notes into structured lessons.
* ✅ **Bilingual Support**: Includes Malayalam meanings for context and clarity.
* ✅ **7-Step Daily Learning**: Covers conversations, grammar, vocab, and practice.
* ✅ **Complete Workflow**: Includes folder structures and content recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started (Day 1)
1.  **Watch**: An English episode or YouTube lesson with English subtitles.
2.  **Note**: Take rough notes of interesting phrases (don't worry about grammar).
3.  **Prompt**: Copy the **Learning Prompt Template** below.
4.  **Process**: Paste your notes into an AI (ChatGPT/Claude).
5.  **Save**: Store the output as `day_01.md`.
6.  **Repeat**: Continue for 180 days.

## 💡 Tips for Success
* **Be Consistent**: Even 15 minutes counts. One day = One note.
* **Speak Aloud**: Don't just read. Mimic the accent and speed of the characters.
* **Avoid Literal Translation**: Focus on how native speakers *feel* and *act*, not just the word-for-word meaning.
* **Review Weekly**: On Sundays, go back through your `day_XX.md` files for that week.

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid
* **Passive Watching**: Don't just sit there; keep your notepad ready.
* **Skipping Grammar**: Understanding the "Why" helps you build your own sentences later.
* **Complex Content**: Avoid shows like *Game of Thrones* or *Sherlock* until you reach an intermediate level.
